Welcome to Rexburg Idaho Chamber of Commerce

NP Membership

NP Membership is for non-profit organizations and is free of charge. It includes access to our online community and discounted rates for special events.

Bronze Membership

Bronze Membership includes all the benefits of the NP Membership, along with the opportunity to attend monthly networking events and training sessions.

Silver Membership

Silver Membership includes all the benefits of Bronze Membership, plus priority registration for special events, advertising opportunities on the chamber's website, and a monthly spotlight position on the home page.

Gold Membership

Gold Membership includes all the benefits of Silver Membership, as well as increased advertising opportunities, premier placement on the website, and access to exclusive chamber events.
