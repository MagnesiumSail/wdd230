The purpose of the Rexburg Chamber of Commerce site is to serve as an online hub for visitors, residents, and businesses. The site will contain attention-grabbing information about the community, and will allow buissness to connect with one another.

  1. What are some local businesses that are members of the chamber, and where can I find a directory of those businesses?
    • This scenario would likely lead the visitor to the Directory page on the site, where they could browse a list of local businesses that belong to the chamber of commerce.
  2. I'm interested in joining the chamber of commerce. What are the benefits of membership, and how can I apply?
    • This scenario would likely lead the visitor to the Join page, where they could learn about the various membership levels and perks, as well as fill out an application form.
  3. I'm visiting Rexburg and want to learn more about the area's history and attractions. Where can I find information about the city's landmarks and events?
    • This scenario would likely lead the visitor to the Discover page, where they could learn about the area's history, demographics, and events, and browse an image montage showcasing local attractions and landmarks.
